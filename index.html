What we spend on war

Methodology & disclaimer ▾
A work of data art and advocacy, not a policy document. Defence spending serves real purposes — deterrence, disaster response, peacekeeping — and many who serve do so with dedication. The investment-gap figures are estimates from peer-reviewed and UN-agency sources; these crises need political will and capacity, not money alone. We have not invented or inflated any figure; all data was verified against primary sources in June 2026.
The world is not simple.
But some of its choices are starker than we allow ourselves to see.
